## Comparison 
When comparing Voyant and ChatGPT as tools for analyzing A Modest Proposal, the main
difference lies in how each tool approaches the text. Voyant focuses on patterns that can be
measured, such as word frequency and distribution, while ChatGPT focuses on interpreting
meaning.

Voyant’s analysis showed that the most frequent words were “children,” “thousand,” “kingdom,”
“country,” and “number.” This tells us that the essay repeatedly refers to children and uses
numerical language. From this data alone, we can already see that counting and statistics are
central to the text. However, Voyant does not explain why Swift uses these repeated words or
what effect they create. It identifies patterns but does not interpret them.

ChatGPT, on the other hand, goes beyond counting words and explains their significance. For
example, while Voyant shows that “thousand” and “number” appear frequently, ChatGPT can
interpret this as part of Swift’s satire. ChatGPT also analyzes tone, irony, and historical context -
elements that Voyant cannot detect.

At the same time, Voyant has strengths that ChatGPT does not automatically provide. Voyant
gives visual and numerical evidence of patterns, which makes claims about repetition more
concrete and measurable. It can show trends over the course of the text, such as how economic
and trade-related terms appear more frequently toward the end. This helps reveal structural
shifts in the essay. However, understanding why that shift matters requires interpretation, which
ChatGPT provides.

## Reflection

This assignment made me think more carefully about what it means when machines “read”
literature for us. What we gain is speed and pattern recognition. Voyant quickly showed me that
words like “children,” “thousand,” and “number” appear over and over again. Through close
reading alone, I might have noticed the economic language, but I probably wouldn’t have
realized how heavily the essay relies on counting and statistics. Seeing that visually made
Swift’s obsession with numbers feel more intentional and structured. It highlighted how much the text frames human lives in economic terms.

But what we lose is depth and judgment. Voyant treats frequency as importance, even though
some of the most powerful moments in A Modest Proposal come from shocking imagery or
ironic phrases that don’t repeat often. GPT, on the other hand, confidently explains themes and
satire, but it sometimes smooths over complexity. It can generalize Swift’s critique or miss subtle
historical tensions unless prompted carefully. It sounds authoritative, but it doesn’t truly “know”
when it might be oversimplifying.

Voyant imagines meaning as measurable patterns. GPT imagines meaning as a coherent
explanation. Combining them helped me see both structure and interpretation, but it also made
me realize that human judgment is still necessary. Only a human reader can decide which
patterns actually matter, recognize irony in context, and question whether an interpretation
makes sense. Machines can assist reading, but they cannot replace the responsibility of making
meaning.
